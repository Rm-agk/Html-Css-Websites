2003-04 "The Invincibles"

The Invincibles is the name given to the Arsenal team which won the 2003-04 Premier League. The reason for the nickname is that they went unbeaten in their 38 league matches. They won 26 games and drew 12. The team is mostly remembered for its attacking genius.
